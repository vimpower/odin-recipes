Lagsana

Ingredent:

  • 10 ounces lasagna noodels
  • 1 1/2 punds lean ground beef
  • 3 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 190 deg C
  2. Bring a large pot of salt water to a boil. Add lagsana noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente, drain the water
  3. In a skilet, brown ground beaf. Using a fork, mix cottage cheese, eggs, garlic powder, parsley, salt and pepper. In a separate bowl combine tomato paste and tomato sauce
  4. In a 9x13 inch baking pan, layer noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce mixture and cottage cheese mixture. Two layers of each will fit. Cover with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove tin foil the last 8 minutes of baking time.
